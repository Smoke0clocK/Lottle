About LOTTLE

I dreamed of a duck named Lottle. It was a little duck. Yellow and squat and wobbly. The Lord called him little Lottle. I asked the Lord about him. The Lord said he will produce the golden egg and as I watched he did just that. He began producing golden eggs. I marveled and said I didn’t know ducks produce golden eggs. Then the Lord said he is really a glorious swan who produces the golden egg. And he then produced opals next, they were translucent and star like in quality; his eggs looked like bright and shiny opals. I fell at Jesus feet and told him I was unworthy for Him to speak to me. I then asked Jesus about when he would produce this Lottle coin and when it would produce? He answered by telling me He would produce enough to care for my tenth generation of descendants. I asked Him if I should share this with all of you? He said if I do He will multiply my inheritance as the dream is meant for me, but sharing it will multiply my returns. As all that is shared comes back to the sharer one hundred fold. Please pray about this and let me know your thoughts! This is not listed on coinmarket cap yet. I am wondering if it is an image of the remnant and what we are to become? Shall we mint a new coin? God bless our little Lottle and our mighty and beautiful Yeshua who shares His dreams and visions with us for the benefit of our tenth generation.
